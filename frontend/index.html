@ Addressing

                    
Direct messages to specific crew in crowded rooms:

                    

                        
@BridgeLumen

                        
@EngineeringAlex

                        
@PersonalDQ

                        
@Science or @MiraMira

                        
@HolodeckHolodeck

                        
@Med or @RynRyn

                        
@Bartender or @RecThe Bartender

                    

                


                

                    
Ship-Wide Announcements

                    
Broadcast to ALL crew at once (crew meetings, alerts):

                    

                        
@allAll crew respond

                        
@shipShip-wide broadcast

                        
@crewAll crew respond

                        
@everyoneAll crew respond

                    

                    
Example: "@all Meet at the bridge in one hour"

                


                

                    
Action Tags

                    
Crew can perform actions by appending tags:

                    

                        
[LOOK]Observe surroundings

                        
[INSPECT: object]Examine something

                        
[DO: action]Free-form action

                        
[MOVE: location]Travel somewhere

                        
[WRITE: "entry"]Journal entry

                        
[THINKING: thought]Inner monologue

                        
[ORDER: drink]Order at bar
